VOLUNTEER WITH US

Make a Difference in Your Community

Volunteers are the heart of our community center. By sharing your time and talents, you can make a meaningful impact in the lives of others while developing new skills and connections.

Why Volunteer With Us?

  • Make a direct impact in your community
  • Develop new skills and gain valuable experience
  • Meet new people and build meaningful relationships
  • Flexible opportunities that fit your schedule
  • Create positive change and help those in need

Volunteer Opportunities

Education Mentor

Help students with homework, teach classes, or assist with after-school programs. Make a difference in a child's educational journey.

2-10 hours per week

Food Bank Assistant

Sort donations, prepare food packages, and help distribute meals to those in need in our community.

3-5 hours per week

Event Coordinator

Help plan, organize, and run community events, workshops, and fundraisers. Perfect for those with organizational skills.

Varies based on events

Digital Skills Coach

Teach basic computer skills, help with job applications, or assist seniors with technology. Share your digital knowledge.

2-4 hours per week

Ready to Get Started?

Fill out our volunteer application form below, and our volunteer coordinator will contact you to discuss opportunities that match your interests and availability.

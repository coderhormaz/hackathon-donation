Frequently Asked Questions

How soon after death should funeral arrangements be made?

For most traditions, arrangements should begin immediately. Islamic funerals typically occur within 24 hours, while other traditions may allow 2-3 days. We're available 24/7 to assist with immediate needs.

What documents are needed to arrange a funeral?

You'll need the death certificate, identification of the deceased, and any pre-arranged funeral documents. For religious services, additional documents like conversion certificates may be required.

Can we customize the funeral service?

Absolutely. We specialize in personalized services that respect both cultural traditions and individual wishes. Our team will work closely with you to create a meaningful ceremony.

Do you handle international repatriation of remains?

Yes, we have extensive experience with international repatriation and can coordinate all necessary arrangements, documentation, and transportation according to different countries' regulations.

Is financial assistance available?

We offer several financial assistance options and payment plans. Our community support fund can help families in need - please contact us to discuss options confidentially.

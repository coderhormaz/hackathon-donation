EDUCATIONAL PROGRAMS

Empowering Through Knowledge

At Urbania Welfare Community Center, we believe that education is a powerful tool for personal growth and community development. Our educational programs are designed to provide accessible learning opportunities for all ages and backgrounds.

Part Time Hifz

Our Part-Time Hifz Program allows students to memorize the Holy Quran while maintaining their regular school or work schedule. This program is ideal for those who want to begin or continue their memorization journey but cannot commit to full-time study..

  • Flexible timing to accommodate school schedules
  • Small class sizes for individual attention
  • Progress tracking meetings
  • Regular parent-teacher meetings
  • Opportunity to participate in Quran competitions
Learn More

Full Time Hifz

Our Full-Time Hifz Program offers a comprehensive, immersive approach to Quran memorization. Students dedicate their full day to Quran studies in a disciplined environment with qualified teachers, structured revision cycles, and personalized guidance to ensure successful completion of memorization.

  • Comprehensive Curriculum
  • Extended Study Hours
  • Memorization Techniques
  • Small Class Size
Learn More

Sunday School

Our Sunday School Program offers a comprehensive, engaging approach to Islamic education. Students attend classes on Sundays in a supportive environment with qualified teachers, structured lessons, and personalized attention to ensure a deep understanding of faith and values.

  • Small Class Sizes

Fityah Program

Our Fityah Program provides an enriching and focused pathway for young Muslims to deepen their faith and knowledge. Students engage in a full-day immersive experience, guided by dedicated instructors, with a structured curriculum, interactive learning methods, and individualized support to foster spiritual growth and understanding.

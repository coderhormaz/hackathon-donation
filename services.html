Food Assistance Program

Our Food Assistance Program aims to alleviate hunger and promote nutrition in our community. We serve individuals and families who may be experiencing food insecurity due to financial hardship, disability, age, or other circumstances.

Weekly Food Pantry

Every Wednesday from 10am to 2pm, community members can visit our food pantry to receive a variety of fresh produce, non-perishable items, dairy products, and protein options. Our goal is to provide nutritionally balanced food options to promote health and well-being.

Emergency Food Boxes

For those facing immediate food needs, we offer emergency food boxes containing essential items to sustain a household for 3-5 days. These can be requested during regular operating hours.

Community Garden

Participate in our community garden program to grow your own produce while learning valuable gardening skills. Plots are available each spring, and excess produce is shared with our food pantry.

Program Hours:

  • Food Pantry: Wednesdays, 10am-2pm
  • Emergency Boxes: Available during center hours
  • Nutrition Classes: Tuesdays, 6pm-7:30pm
  • Senior Meal Delivery: Monday, Wednesday, Friday

Housing Support Program

Our Housing Support Program helps individuals and families secure and maintain safe, stable housing. We provide guidance through the complex housing process and connect clients with resources to prevent homelessness.

Housing Search Assistance

Our housing specialists help clients identify affordable housing options, complete applications, and understand lease agreements. We maintain relationships with local landlords and property managers to expand housing opportunities.

Eviction Prevention

For those at risk of losing their housing, we offer intervention services including rental assistance, mediation with landlords, and connections to legal resources.

Utility Assistance

Help with utility bills, energy efficiency resources, and connections to assistance programs to keep utilities on and homes comfortable throughout the year.

Program Hours:

  • Housing Consultations: Monday-Thursday, 9am-5pm
  • Rental Assistance Applications: Tuesdays and Thursdays, 10am-3pm
  • Housing Workshops: First Saturday of each month, 10am-12pm
